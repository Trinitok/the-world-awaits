Your Birthday

The Day Planned for You

Noon
— 2pm
🍽 Brunch · Pendry Chicago

Venteux Brasserie & Oyster Bar

A Parisian brasserie nestled in the iconic Carbide & Carbon building on Michigan Avenue. I want to take you back in time and re-imagine the sunlight through 40-foot windows, velvet booths, fresh oysters, and French onion soup. A French string group playing in the background. Memories of your former trip to Paris swimming back

2pm
— 3:30pm
🏛 Museum · Near North Side

Driehaus Museum

After lunch we step inside the stunning Nickerson Mansion — nicknamed "the Marble Palace" — for an immersive journey through Gilded Age splendor. Seventeen types of marble, Louis Comfort Tiffany masterpieces, and stories from 1883 that feel impossibly alive. Here we can imagine walking through the European streets, admiring the statues and old fashioned architecture together.

3:30pm
— 5pm
🔭 Views · 875 N Michigan Ave

360 Chicago Observation Deck

Together we can watch over the entirety of Chicago from 1000 feet in the air. We can relax and have a drink while laughing and enjoying each others company. Looking out over the horizon and imagining the future together

5pm
— 7pm
✨ Downtime · The Loop

Rest & (OPTIONAL) Museum of Illusions

In case we need a break after walking or being social all day. A walk by the Chicago River before dinner. Perhaps a brief nap. Maybe a visit to the Museum of Illusions for a laugh. All before dinner and the conclusion of the day

7pm
🥂 Dinner · St. Regis Chicago, 11th Floor

Miru at The St. Regis

The grand finale: Miru — meaning "view" in Japanese — sits on the 11th floor of the St. Regis with panoramic terraces overlooking the Chicago River, Navy Pier, and Lake Michigan. Chef Hisanobu Osaka's Japanese-inspired cuisine: wagyu, fresh sashimi, maki, and cocktails that taste like a dream.
