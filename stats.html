Planner Notes & Pro Tips

  • Leave Logan Square by ~9:45am to pick her up in Mt. Prospect and still arrive at Venteux comfortably by noon.
  • Book Driehaus tickets online in advance — timed entry helps avoid waits. The guided tour is highly rated and worth the $7.
  • 360 Chicago: buy tickets online beforehand to skip the ticket desk line. Afternoon timing (3:30–5pm) is great for pre-sunset light.
  • Museum of Illusions: pre-book online at moichicago.com — walk-up can be ~$34/person vs ~$22 online in advance.
  • Miru reservation is critical — request a terrace or window table overlooking the river. Book well in advance on OpenTable.
  • Uber surge pricing is possible around 5pm and again post-dinner (~10pm). Budget the higher end of each estimate to be safe.
  • Weather contingency: If it rains, the walk legs (Venteux→Driehaus, Driehaus→360) become Ubers. Budget an extra ~$25–35.
  • The 3.5% surcharge appears on both Venteux and Miru checks per their menus — factor into tip calculations.
  • Total drive time for the day: ~1.5–2 hours driving (Mt. Prospect round trip). Everything downtown is walkable or a short Uber.
